# DHCC Services and Specialties

## Medical Specialties Available

DHCC offers services across virtually all medical specialties, including:

- Internal Medicine
- General Surgery
- Pediatrics
- Obstetrics and Gynecology
- Oncology
- Cardiology
- Orthopedics
- Neurology and Neurosurgery
- Dentistry and Cosmetic Dentistry
- Ophthalmology
- Dermatology
- Fertility and IVF
- Rehabilitation and Physiotherapy
- Pulmonology
- ENT (Ear, Nose, Throat)
- Radiology and Interventional Radiology
- Psychiatry and Psychology
- Nutrition and Dietetics

## Popular Procedures for Medical Tourists

Common treatments sought at DHCC include:

- **Infertility treatments (IVF)**
- **Cosmetic surgery**
- **Dental procedures** (implants, veneers, whitening)
- **Cardiac procedures** (angioplasty, cardiac ablation)
- **Orthopedic surgery** (joint replacement, sports injuries)
- **Ophthalmology** (LASIK, retina treatment)

## Centers of Excellence

- Pediatric cardiology at Al Jalila Children's Hospital
- Spine and neuroscience at Emirates Specialty Hospital
- Eye care at Moorfields Eye Hospital Dubai
- Multi-specialty tertiary care at Mediclinic City Hospital and Dr. Sulaiman Al Habib Hospital

## Telemedicine

DHCC actively supports telehealth. During and after COVID-19, hospitals and clinics offer video consultations across licensed specialties. Okadoc's telemedicine platform connects patients with 400+ doctors across 40+ specialties with online billing.

## Booking Channels

Patients can book through:

1. DHCC Find a Doctor portal (Okadoc-powered) on dhcc.ae
2. Individual hospital websites and phone lines
3. Direct hospital reception desks

## Insurance

Most DHCC hospitals accept major UAE insurance plans (Daman, AXA Gulf, Bupa Global, Allianz, Cigna) and many international plans. Patients should verify coverage for specific procedures with their insurer and the hospital billing office.

## Structured Data Reference

Doctor and clinic details are maintained in structured CSV files used by the voice agent tools:

- `doctors.csv` — practitioner directory with specialties, languages, and clinic affiliations
- `clinics.csv` — hospital and clinic directory with addresses, hours, and contact info
