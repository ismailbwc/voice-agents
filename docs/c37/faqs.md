# C37 Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### What is C37?

C37 is a private medical co-working space managed by DHCC. It provides turnkey clinic space for independent physicians — not direct hospital care.

### Is C37 a hospital?

No. C37 provides consulting rooms and support services. Major procedures and emergencies are handled at partner DHCC hospitals.

## Appointments

### How do I book an appointment with a C37 doctor?

Contact C37 reception at +971 4 383 8333, use the doctor's profile on c37.ae, or ask the virtual receptionist to help find and book a doctor.

### Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes. Contact C37 reception to cancel or reschedule. Standard clinic policies apply (24-hour notice recommended).

## Doctors

### Which specialties are available at C37?

Pediatrics, internal medicine, aesthetics, plastic surgery, radiology, dietetics, audiology, speech therapy, and general practice. See the doctor directory for the full list.

### Do C37 doctors also work at hospitals?

Many C37 physicians maintain privileges at DHCC hospitals. For example, Dr. Yaser Naji practices at Clemenceau Medical Center alongside his C37 membership.

## Insurance

### Does C37 accept insurance?

Yes. C37 processes billing through major UAE and international insurance networks on behalf of member doctors. Confirm your specific plan when booking.

### Is malpractice insurance included?

Yes. All C37 doctors are covered by medical malpractice insurance coordinated through the center.

### What are C37 opening hours?

Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM. Saturday by appointment. Closed Sunday.

## For Physicians

### How do I join C37 as a doctor?

Submit an enquiry on c37.ae. C37 assists with DHA licensing, visa processing, and malpractice insurance setup.

### What is included in membership?

Private office/exam room, nursing and admin staff, appointment management, billing support, insurance network access, IT/EHR support, and licensing assistance. See membership.md for details.

## Emergencies

### What do I do in a medical emergency?

Call **999** for ambulance service or go to the nearest DHCC hospital emergency department. C37 does not have an emergency department.
