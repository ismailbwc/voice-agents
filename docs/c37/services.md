# C37 Services

## Clinical Services Available Through C37 Doctors

C37 member physicians offer outpatient consultations and minor procedures across these specialties:

- Pediatrics and Pediatric Pulmonology
- General Practice and Family Medicine
- Internal Medicine (Endocrinology, Rheumatology, Integrative Medicine)
- Aesthetic Medicine and Anti-Aging
- Plastic and Cosmetic Surgery
- Interventional Radiology
- Clinical Dietetics and Nutrition
- Audiology and Vestibular Rehabilitation
- Speech and Language Therapy
- Beauty Therapy and Micropigmentation

## What Happens On-Site

- Private consultations and follow-up visits
- Minor diagnostics (blood tests, basic ultrasounds via partner labs)
- Aesthetic procedures (Botox, fillers, PRP, IV wellness therapy)
- Pediatric examinations and spirometry
- Dietary counseling and nutrition planning
- Hearing assessments and vestibular rehab

## Referrals to Partner Hospitals

For advanced imaging, surgery, or hospitalization, C37 doctors refer patients to DHCC partner hospitals:

- Mediclinic City Hospital
- Dr. Sulaiman Al Habib Hospital
- Clemenceau Medical Center
- Emirates Specialty Hospital

## Telemedicine

C37 doctors may offer telehealth consultations through DHCC-approved platforms (Okadoc). C37 does not operate its own telemedicine platform.

## International Collaborations

C37 has strategic partnerships bringing visiting specialists to the UAE:

- Canadian Specialist Hospital (2022 MOU)
- Sir Ganga Ram Hospital — Institute of Vascular Sciences, India (2026)
