# C37 — Overview

## What is C37?

C37 is the UAE's first private medical co-working space, launched in 2021 and fully managed by Dubai Healthcare City (DHCC). It is designed for physicians — both UAE-based and visiting from abroad — who want to run an independent, part-time clinical practice without the overhead of building their own clinic.

C37 is **not a traditional healthcare provider**. It is an enabler that provides turnkey medical workspace so doctors can focus on patient care.

## What C37 Provides

- Fully equipped private offices and consulting rooms
- On-site nursing and administrative staff
- Appointment scheduling and patient registration
- Billing, collections, and insurance network access
- Medical malpractice insurance coverage
- DHA licensing and visa support for visiting specialists
- IT infrastructure and EHR support
- Discounted access to partner diagnostic labs

## Locations

C37 operates in two DHCC phases:

- **C37 Oud Metha** — Phase 1, Bur Dubai
- **C37 Al Jaddaf** — Phase 2, Al Jaddaf

## Physician Network

C37 hosts approximately 25–30 resident physicians across specialties including pediatrics, aesthetics, internal medicine, radiology, plastic surgery, dietetics, audiology, and speech therapy.

## Patient Experience

Patients can book appointments with C37 doctors through:

- C37 reception (+971 4 383 8333)
- Individual doctor profiles on c37.ae
- DHCC Find a Doctor portal

Major procedures and hospital admissions are typically referred to partner DHCC hospitals (Mediclinic City, Dr. Sulaiman Al Habib, Clemenceau Medical Center, etc.).

## What the Virtual Receptionist Can Help With

### Patients
- Finding C37 doctors by specialty
- Booking consultations with C37 doctors (demo)
- Providing facility locations and hours
- Answering insurance and billing questions

### Physicians (members / visiting doctors)
- Explaining membership benefits
- Booking consulting rooms, exam rooms, and private offices (demo)
- Daily, weekly, or monthly workspace reservations
- Directions to Oud Metha and Al Jaddaf facilities
- Explaining how C37 connects to the broader DHCC ecosystem

## Important Boundaries

C37 reception cannot provide medical diagnoses. For emergencies, call **999**. C37 does not operate its own emergency department — patients are referred to DHCC partner hospitals.
