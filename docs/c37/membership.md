# C37 Membership Benefits

## Who Can Join C37?

C37 is designed for:

- Overseas physicians wanting part-time practice in Dubai
- UAE-based specialists seeking flexible clinic space
- Visiting consultants needing a turnkey clinical environment

## Membership Includes

### Clinic Facilities
- Private offices and fully equipped exam rooms
- Central waiting area and reception desk
- Modern medical furnishings and Wi-Fi

### Support Staff
- On-site nurses and administrators
- Receptionists managing patient intake and scheduling

### Operations
- Patient registration and appointment management
- Billing, collections, and insurance processing
- Medical malpractice insurance coverage
- Access to major UAE and international insurance networks

### Licensing and Visa
- DHA professional licensing assistance
- Free-zone employment visa processing for visiting doctors
- Immigration and business setup support

### Technology
- Hospital information systems and EHR support
- On-site IT support
- Discounted diagnostic lab network access

## Membership Model

C37 operates on a subscription / pay-as-you-go model. Doctors book space as needed (daily, weekly, or monthly) without upfront clinic build-out costs.

## How to Apply

Physicians can apply via the enquiry form on c37.ae or contact C37 directly at +971 4 383 8333.

## Physician Networking

C37 enables doctors to network with hospitals and clinics across the DHCC ecosystem, facilitating referrals and collaborative care.
